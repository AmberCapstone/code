 OV7670 | Arduino
--------+---------
 3v3    | 3v3
 GND    | GND
 SCL    | A5
 SDA    | A4
 VSYNC  | D3 (INT1)
 HREF   | D8
 PCLK   | D2 (INT0)
 XCLK   | D11
 D[7:4] | D[7:4]
 D[3:0] | A[3:0]
 RESET  | 3v3
 PWDN   | GND

 Notes
-------
D[7:0], VSYNC, HREF, PCLK are 3.3V outputs from OV7670
and need a 3.3V to 5V step up converter, such as TXS0108E

XCLK output from Arduino is 5V and should be stepped
down to 3.3 with a 4.7k + 10k resistor divider.

SCL and SDA should be pulled to 3.3V with 10k.
